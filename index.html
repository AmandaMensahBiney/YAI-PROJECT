Meet Afia Acheampomaa Adu!

Miss Afia, a dynamic entrepreneur from Tema in the Greater Accra Region seeks to revolutionalize logistics and surveillance in Ghana through innovative drone solutions. Passionate about technology and efficiency, she founded a drone delivery service to tackle poor delivery systems while enhancing industrial inspections with advanced aerial mapping and monitoring.
Operating at the intersection of Technology and Logistics, her venture seeks to streamline supply chain operations, improve last-mile delivery, and support industries with precision surveillance. Her commitment to innovation aims at driving efficiency and technological advancement in Ghana’s logistics landscape.
